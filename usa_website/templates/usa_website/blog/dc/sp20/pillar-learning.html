Pillar Learning

Improving User Retention for AI-enabled Educational Toy

PM: Gwyneth Ang

Project Description

Pillar Learning develops AI-enabled educational storyteller toys for children. Their mission is to leverage innovative technology and educational research to meet the developmental needs of every child. Their main product, Codi, is a screenless AI-enabled toy that provides children with curated classic songs and stories to provide developmental support outside of the classroom. The current goal of this project is to analyze user behavior data, such as the amount of time spent on various features by children aged 0-7, and make recommendations for improving user retention.
