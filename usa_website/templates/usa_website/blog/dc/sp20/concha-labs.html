Concha Labs

A.I. Hearing Loss Diagnosis

PM: Haoming Guo



Project Description

Concha Labs is a startup at Skydeck focused on personalized hearing aids, and we are helping them better estimate their users’ hearing profile. In the past, people have designed tests to estimate a person’s hearing loss curve, but we are building an active learning algorithm that designs such test interactively. Through a series of machine-generated tests, we hope to approximate a user’s real hearing loss curve as much as possible. This project will combine active learning, experiment design, simulations, and possibly deep learning.
