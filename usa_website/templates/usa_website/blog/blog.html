Journal Introduction

The Statistics Undergraduate Students Association is proud to announce The University Statistics Review, our online journal to showcase undergraduate statistics and data science-related research. Throughout the semester, students have used statistical methods and tools to analyze a myriad of topics of personal interest, from song lyrics to the game of Go. The wide-ranging topics of articles we produce reflect the diversity of the statistics community itself, and we aim to publish content accessible to all readers. In addition, this journal serves as an outlet for discussion and commentary on the statistics community at Berkeley, whether it be experiences or ongoing developments. Articles are intended to be accessible to those without a statistical background, in order to foster broader interdisciplinary discourse. Readers can anticipate data-driven articles and more — coming soon.
