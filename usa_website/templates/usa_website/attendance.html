The Point System

This year, SUSA will be debuting our new Attendance System! Throughout the semester, there will be lots of points available to all SUSA members. The threshold to reach by the end of the semester will be 100 points - this will be taken into consideration when we consider returning member applications at the end of the semester. There will likely be other events and opportunities to earn extra points throughout the semester, but the minimum threshold will not change. Members can earn points by coming to SUSA related activities and events ranging from workshops held by our Education committee to tech talks hosted by our EVP committee and socials held by our IVP committee. In addition to these events, your committee director will be allotted 25 "Director's Discretion" points to award for comittee socials or other activities. Be sure to ask your director how they will distribute these points!
