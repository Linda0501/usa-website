Career Exploration Committee


Career Exploration

The Career Exploration Committee is designed to build a strong statistics and data science foundation among its members. As a member of the committee you will participate in a weekly lecture and get the chance to work on team projects that apply the concepts taught in lecture. Towards the end of the semester, you will compete in a committee-wide Kaggle data analysis competition, allowing you to showcase your newfound knowledge of machine learning techniques including linear regression, random forests, and neural networks.
