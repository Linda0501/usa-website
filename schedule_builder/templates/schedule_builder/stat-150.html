Stat 150

Statistics 150, Stochastic Processes, is a meant as a second course in probability theory. Stochastic processes model a range of random phenomena over time and space. Topics include branching processes, Markov chains, martingales, renewal processes, Poisson processes and Brownian motion. These are the building blocks of many of the probabilistic models used in applications.
