Faculty Advisors

Renowned Statistics Professors at the UC Berkeley Statistics Deparment act as faculty advisors to support and give our officers advice.

David Aldous

Evans 351

Professor David Aldous received his Ph.D. from Cambridge University and has worked at Berkeley since 1979. His research in probability has covered Markov chains, random networks, and stochastic coalescence. In addition, he teaches several upper division statistics courses and is a Fellow of the Royal Society and a foreign associate of the National Academy of Sciences.

Joan Bruna

Evans 419

Assistant Professor Joan Bruna earned his Ph.D. in applied mathematics from École Polytechnique. His primary research interest is machine learning. He joined Berkeley in 2015 and previously worked for an image processing startup, the machine learning group at NYU, and Facebook AI Research.

Adityanand Guntuboyina

Evans 423

Assistant Professor Adityanand Guntuboyina recieved his Ph.D in statistics and has worked at Berkeley since 2012 after finishing his post-doc at the University of Pennsylvania. He is currently working on statistical problems involving convexity typfied by the estimation of convex sets and functions from various types of noisy measurements.



Executive Team

The Statistics Undergraduate Student Association is led by a group of statistics enthusiasts who have great passion for promote the statistics major to all students in UC Berkeley. They come from different backgrounds and are interested in different areas within statistics.

Arun Ramamurthy

President

The President of our club oversees all of our club operations, as well as push for new initiatives. Across Arun’s two-year career in USA, he has been a member of Events and Projects committees, in addition to being the director of Projects for the last two semesters.

Adish Jain

External Vice President

Our External Vice President deals with outreach to the wider campus community, as well as to other organizations and companies that may be interested in networking with our members. Before becoming the EVP, Adish spent a semester as a member of the Career Exploration committee and another as the director of that committee.

Isabelle Townley-Smith

Internal Vice President

Our Internal Vice President keeps our club and the statistics community on campus updated about club events, and deals with general internal affairs. Isabelle has been a part of the Events and Career Exploration committees before becoming IVP.


Oscar Syu

Financial Vice President

Our Financial Vice President is responsible for our club finances, including budgeting for events, organizing fundraisers, and coordinating with the ASUC. Oscar spent his first semester with the club in the Events committee before becoming the External Vice President.
